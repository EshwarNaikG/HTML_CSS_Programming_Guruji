Background position

The background-position property in CSS is used to specify the position of a background image within an element. It allows you to control where the background image is placed in relation to the element's content and edges. The background-position property can take various values, including keywords, length units, and percentage values.

Here are some common values for the background-position property:

  • top left
  • top center
  • top right
  • center left
  • center center
  • center right
  • bottom left
  • bottom center
  • bottom right
